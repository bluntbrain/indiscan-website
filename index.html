About the campaign

It all started when Indian Intelligence Agency released a list of 53 applications that knownligly invades our mobile security and privacy. Data breached by such applications are used to create individual profiles and then sold in packages to other businesses and governments.
Enough is enough! Privacy is the qualified, fundamental human right and absolutely no one can invade it. We are calling every Indian to stand against the current India-China tensions. Take the pledge today, delete Chinese applications from your device and install their alternatives. Together, let's create awareness among friends and family, let the moment spread lights online threats and protection against malwares and unauthorised applications.
